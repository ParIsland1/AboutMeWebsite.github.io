Personal Interests

The Gym: One of my favorite things to do everyday is go to the gym. For me, it is less for physical well being and health as it is for fun. The gym is a place where I get to unwind and try to break my own records and challenge myself everyday. I enjoy going with friends whenever I can but mostly go by myself.

Personal Projects: Another hobby of mine is creating personal projects often involving the computer. I like to make games, or just random programs for fun. I do not think I am very good at either but, with time, I hope I will be one day. Taking classes at UVU will help me improve and I enjoy my "toolbox" being added to whenever I learn something new.

Rock Climbing: And finally a hobby I have had since I was a lot younger is rock climbing. My brother and I are avid fans of rock climbing, bouldering specifically. I grew up in Southern California so we would often go to Joshua Tree National Park and scamper around the rocks there. We also were members of a rock climbing gym back home.
